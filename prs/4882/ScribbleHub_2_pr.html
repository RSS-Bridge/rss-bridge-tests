    by: SublimeEQ

    Rituals of Flesh and Consciousness is a collection of intimate short stories that explore the fragile boundary between body and mind, desire and fear, presence and memory. Through quiet moments, restrained gestures, and unspoken emotions, the work examines how intimacy can become a ritual — not of excess, but of awareness.

    Each narrative focuses on connection as an internal experience: the weight of a breath, the warmth of proximity, the silence shared between two bodies in a closed space. There is no spectacle, only perception. What unfolds is not merely closeness, but the realization of being seen without judgment, untouched by external eyes.

    The book invites the reader to reflect on vulnerability as a form of strength, and on intimacy as an act of consciousness — a meeting where the body listens as much as it feels.
