NONE - Dusk Skies Over The Dreaming Forests

Track V from the long anticipated and fabled album "Supreme Dedication To The Craft Is Forever".

"Dusk Skies Over The Dreaming Forests" is the first song written for None by Nicholas Mendiola, The Great None, and The True None - in the year 2015 AD; via the Gregorian Calendar.

Album version recorded and performed by Nicholas Mendiola and John Schuler between the years 2022 and 2024; engineered by Ryan Perras in San Jose, California.

Video filmed and edited by John Schuler and Nicholas Mendiola in the Santa Cruz Mountains, August of 2024.

The True None sings The Dark Gospel, exclusively.
Never Just Music.
