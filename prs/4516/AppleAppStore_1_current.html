WhatsApp Messenger - 25.2.3

2025-01-28 00:00:00

by: WhatsApp Inc.

• You can now dial phone numbers without saving them to your contacts. Tap the “+” icon on the Calls tab and select “Call a number” to open the dialer.
• You can now select participants prior to starting a group call. Tap the phone or video icon in your group chat, then tap the group name to select people to call.
• You can now add people you are chatting with to an ongoing call. Tap the phone or video icon in a 1:1 chat to add this person to the call.



These features will roll out over the coming weeks. Thanks for using WhatsApp!

WhatsApp Messenger - 25.1.2

2025-01-16 00:00:00

by: WhatsApp Inc.

• New gestures include double tap to quickly react to a message and pinch to zoom your camera during video calls.
• Sharing multiple images and videos now sends as a collection.
• You can now scan documents with your camera and send as a document. Tap ‘+’ in chat > ‘Document’ > ‘Scan document’ to get started.
• You can now add AR effects, backgrounds and filters from the Camera. Open Camera and tap the ‘wand’ icon to get started.
• You can now keep the unread message count on your app icon after you leave the app. Go to Settings > 'Notifications' and turn off 'Clear badge' under 'Home screen notifications'.



These features will roll out over the coming weeks. Thanks for using WhatsApp!
