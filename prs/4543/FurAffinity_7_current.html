by: KatfishCom

guyde from , text by
Hello everybody! I'm sorry to inform you that it's no longer possible to transfer payment for commissions through Buy Me a Coffee. The option to withdraw funds with EasyStart is no longer available due to new company policies.

HOWEVER! EasyStart is not only a facilitator but also a viable payment method. They do not collect or store your personal data. It's completely safe to use and me and other freelancers can attest to that.
EasyStart also has a very good customer support. You'll always get a timely response from an actual person.
If you want to ask them about something you can go here: http://easystaff.io

To transfer money through EasyStart you don't have to sign up for anything. You just confirm your identity with Google and fill out a form with your full name. After that I will be able to send you an invoice with a Stripe link that supports payment with debit card/link/Gpay/iDeal.
There's no commission fee and I'll receive the payment very quickly. Currency conversion is also automatic!

Please share this post, tell other artists or your clients about this! It's very important to have alternative and safe payment methods available, and I want to inform others about them.
It's still possible to use Boosty for this kind of thing, but the amount of errors and technical difficulties is unacceptable. We're trying really hard to find ways to make the process of commissioning artwork more comfortable for us and our clients.

If you've already used EasyStart please share your experience with it (you're an artist or a client) in the comments so more people can trust this service.
Thank you for your understanding!
