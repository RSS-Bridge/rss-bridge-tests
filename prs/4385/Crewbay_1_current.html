Sailing the Greek Islands

I am sailing around Greece over summer around Athens, kea, epidavros, Aegina, Mykonos, hydra, Pylos,Santorini, and crete and many other places... Maybe turkey too.

I can sail the yacht mostly by myself as the yacht is new and highly automated (autopilot, furlers, ais, remote windlass, mantis storm anchor, lazy jack main, davits...), so help is mostly keeping watch and mooring in marinas and ports. Plus life is always better shared.

I have been sailing around Italy, France, Greece since 2014, done over 10,000 sea miles now. I've been sailing since I was 8 years old. I am also a pretty good cook and the yacht has a BBQ and kitchen etc. I was a certified sailing instructor so I can teach you for free. Shared costs are negotiable and rather small, as island hopping costs are minimal as I like anchoring and cooking...

Please note that this yacht is 8m, there are 4 separate bunks/beds but no separate cabins, however the rear guest cabin has privacy.
Max crew is 2.

Ideally looking for crew that likes sailing, swimming, sightseeing ancient sites and Italian cuisine. Ideally looking for people that are fun, honest and reliable. Experience is not needed as I can teach you. Minimum stay is a week, max is 2 months. Day sails are also possible.

SMALL YACHT = LESS COSTS. Less than 2- 10 euros a day for your share of marina, fuel and food. However I like to anchor where possible as you can't swim in ports.

For further pics of previous sailing trips, my cooking, yacht and me, message to make a request. Happy sailing...
